Our Story

Specialists in Desi
Clothing & Fit

With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in tailoring and alterations for South Asian clothing. From sarees and salwar kameez to blouse fittings and resizing, each piece is handled with care and precision.

We understand the fit, style, and details that Desi clothing requires, and focus on making sure every outfit looks and feels right — for your special occasion, or everyday elegance.

What We Do

Our Services

01
Saree & Lehenga Work

Expert blouse stitching, saree fall & piko, lace & border additions, and full lehenga construction or restyling.

02
Salwar Kameez Tailoring

Custom stitching of kameez, salwar, and dupatta sets from your fabric — sized perfectly to your measurements.

03
Blouse Fitting

Precision blouse fitting, back neckline adjustments, sleeve alterations, and custom cut work for the perfect silhouette.

04
Resizing & Alterations

Taking in or letting out existing outfits, hemming, waist adjustments, and full resizing for outfits passed down or bought abroad.

05
Bridal & Occasion Wear

Bridal outfit preparation, heavy work finishing, embroidery reinforcement, and final fitting for your most important day.

06
Kurta & Sherwani Tailoring

Men's kurta and sherwani stitching, collar work, button detailing, and alterations for a sharp, tailored look.
